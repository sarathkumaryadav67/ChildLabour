WEEKLY REPORT

10-06-2024 to 15-06-2024

Objective of the Activity Done:

To know about causes of child labour.

DETAILED REPORT:

DAY 1: In the fifth week, we gathered information about children in our areas.

DAY 2: We raised some questions about child labour and interacted with children.

DAY 3: On this day, we decided to talk about the causes of child labour:

  • Lack of access to quality education.
  • Poor access to decent work.

DAY 4: On this day, we raised the question, “What is the solution to child labour?” and created a dialogue with children and families to send children to school, and provide admissions assistance.

DAY 5: On this day, we discussed the types of child labour:

  • Child labour in agriculture.
  • Child labour in armed conflict.
  • Trafficking in children.
  • Mining and quarrying.
  • Child labour in domestic work.

DAY 6: We discussed the effects of child labour:

  • Illiteracy
  • Lost childhood
  • Mental health issues
